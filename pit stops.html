Isle of Man TT Races

 

Pit Stops:

The timing of what lap to pit is a major strategic challenge. Too much time spent in the pit stop can lose precious seconds on the track.

In the Senior TT 6-lap races, the racers pit after the 2nd and 4th lap and take on fuel and get a new rear tire. A crew of four is allowed over the wall, fuel is loaded via gravity feed, and 1 person to change the rear tire.
